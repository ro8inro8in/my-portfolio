    • ABOUT ME!

    I am a freelance audio editor, my experience in the music industry spans over ten years. To begin with, my Dad taught me how to play guitar, later I moved on to college to further my education in musical composition. When I finished college I went on to university, I moved my focus to music production and sound engineering. I have worked on many projects in various aspects utilising all my skills with a main focus on live performance and production, This continued to evolve year after year until recently where I spent two years touring around Europe, unfortunately to the demise of the band we all stopped. That brings me to now where I am a working as a freelance Audio editor.
