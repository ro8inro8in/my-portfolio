    • ABOUT ME!

    Hi, I am a musician. When I was roughly 12 years old, my Dad taught me how to play the Guitar. I persued this threw out high school and moved on to college where I furthered my education in musical composition. When I finished college I went on to university and moved my focus into music production.

    WHY CODE?
